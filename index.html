about

Serving Saint Francis of Assisi Parish and the Litchfield NH Community

Founded in 2001, Litchfield NH Council 13026 follows the Knights of Columbus' principles of Charity, Unity, Fraternity and Patriotism which serve as the basis of the Order's core values of Integrity, Professionalism, Excellence, and Respect.

The Council conducts a business meeting at Saint Francis Church and School, 9 Saint Francis Way Litchfield,NH. Meetings are held on the 2nd Thursday of the month at 7PM (School) with a Rosary said at 6:30PM (Church) before the meeting. Dues are $25.00 per year.

The Council hosts a large number of activities throughout the year, including Church Activities, Youth Activities, Family Activities, Community Service and Council Activities.

For more than 20 years the Council has been active in the Saint Francis Parish and the Litchfield NH Community, performing a wide range of charitable acts. It has been through the support and generosity of the Parishioners of Saint Francis that we have been successful. We offer our sincerest thanks and appreciation to them for all they do!
